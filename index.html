About

Founded by someone who understands healthcare

FigCare Systems was founded by Daniel Figueroa, BSN, RN, with a background rooted in healthcare from the beginning. Inspired early on by his mother’s work in the medical field, Daniel went on to work as a medical assistant before becoming a registered nurse.

His clinical background includes oncology, bone marrow transplant, step-down care, and travel nursing. That experience shaped the way FigCare Systems approaches technology support for medical practices: with professionalism, urgency, discretion, and respect for how important reliable systems are in healthcare.

FigCare Systems serves Bay Area medical offices with the goal of helping practices stay secure, efficient, and supported behind the scenes so doctors and staff can stay focused on caring for patients and running the practice.

My goal is simple: provide dependable, professional support that helps medical offices run more smoothly, more securely, and with less stress.
